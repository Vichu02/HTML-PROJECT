CHRIST NAGAR COLLEGE

ABOUT US

Buoyed by the unstinting support extended by the public to Christ Nagar Group of Institutions, Christ Nagar College, affiliated to University of Kerala, Thiruvananthapuram, was established by CMI Fathers, the pioneers in the field of education and social reforms in the year 2012 at Maranalloor, a sleepy hamlet in Thiruvananthapuram District just 12 kms away from the city limits.
"To be a National leader in transforming lives through an excellent, innovative, radical, rigorous, relevant and compassionate approach to education"
